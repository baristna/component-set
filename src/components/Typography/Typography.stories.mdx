### Style Guide
#### Type Scale
There are 9 styles in the new typography set.
Most body text in tables, forms, etc, will use the Body 2 style (14px), large blocks of body copy use Body 1 (16px)
while primary header will be Subtitle 1 and 2 styles (20px).

Title can be used for emphasis on large body copy sections, or empty screens.

Legend corresponds to the form Legends titles,
Captions are text fields labels.

Medium weight variants can be used for Body 1 and 2 for emphasis and when using white text on dark backgrounds.

Supported properties <a href='https://styled-system.com/api/#typography'>here</a>
